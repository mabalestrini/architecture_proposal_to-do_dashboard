# Color Standards

## Principle

Area colors and Zone colors serve different purposes.

- Area colors visually identify major operational domains.
- Zone colors indicate exposure posture.

Color is a soft semantic cue, not an alarm system.

Use:
- pastel colors
- low saturation
- professional readability
- print-friendly contrasts

Avoid:
- saturated red
- SOC-style alarm visuals
- excessive color density

## Zone Exposure Colors

| Zone Exposure | Meaning | Color Direction |
|---|---|---|
| Low Exposure | Least exposed / most protected | Green family |
| Medium Exposure | Intermediate exposure | Yellow family |
| High Exposure | Most exposed | Orange family |
| Administrative Exposure | Privileged management plane | Lavender/purple family |
| Controlled NONPROD | Lab / isolated testing | Mint/teal family |
